Our Legacy

15 Years of Excellence in Timber & Craftsmanship

Rooted in Strength

Since 2010, R P GOYAL & SONS has been more than just a timber merchant; we are the guardians of quality.

What started as a modest family depot in Karnal, Haryana has grown into a premier timber powerhouse. We believe that every log tells a story, and our job is to ensure that story ends in a beautiful home, a sturdy building, or a piece of fine furniture.

We source only the finest Teak, Sal, and Meranti, personally inspecting every batch to guarantee it meets our "Gold Standard" of durability.
